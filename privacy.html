Information We Collect

+

We collect information to provide better services to our pet training clients. The types of information we gather include:

Personal Information

Information you provide directly when booking services or contacting us.

  • Contact Details: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address
  • Pet Information: Pet names, breeds, ages, behavioral concerns, and training history
  • Service Preferences: Training programs of interest, scheduling preferences, and special requirements
  • Payment Information: Billing details processed securely through encrypted payment systems
  • Communication Records: Messages, emails, and notes from consultations

Automatically Collected Information

Technical data collected when you visit our website or use our services.

  • Website Usage: Pages visited, time spent, and navigation patterns
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
  • Location Data: General location for service area determination (with consent)

How We Use Your Information

+

Your information helps us deliver personalized pet training services and improve our offerings:

  • Service Delivery: Schedule appointments, conduct training sessions, and provide follow-up support
  • Communication: Send appointment confirmations, training tips, and service updates
  • Personalization: Tailor training programs to your pet's specific needs and behaviors
  • Payment Processing: Handle transactions securely and maintain billing records
  • Service Improvement: Analyze feedback to enhance our training methods and customer experience
  • Legal Compliance: Meet regulatory requirements and protect against fraud

Marketing Communications

With your consent, we may send training tips, success stories, and information about new services. You can opt-out anytime.

Information Sharing & Disclosure

+

We respect your privacy and limit information sharing to essential business purposes:

We DO NOT sell your personal information

Your data is never sold, rented, or traded to third parties for marketing purposes.

Limited sharing occurs only in these situations:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help deliver our services (payment processors, scheduling systems)
  • Veterinary Professionals: With your explicit consent, we may share relevant information with your vet
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect safety
  • Business Transfers: In the unlikely event of a business sale or merger (with notice to you)
  • Emergency Situations: To protect the health and safety of pets, owners, or staff

All third parties we work with are required to maintain the same level of data protection we provide.

Data Security & Protection

+

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

Technical Safeguards

Industry-standard security protocols protect your data from unauthorized access.

  • Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Secure Servers: Data stored on protected servers with regular security updates
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Staff Training: All team members trained on privacy protection and data handling

Your Security Role

Help us protect your information by using strong passwords and keeping your login credentials secure.

Your Privacy Rights

+

You have full control over your personal information. Your rights include:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal information we have about you
  • Correction: Update or correct any inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Portability: Receive your data in a standard format for transfer to another service
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your information in certain circumstances

How to Exercise Your Rights

Contact us using the information below. We'll respond to all requests within 30 days and verify your identity for security.

Managing Cookies: You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may limit website functionality.
