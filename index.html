Daily Training Tips

1

Consistency is Key

Practice the same commands daily for 5-10 minutes. Regular short sessions are more effective than long, infrequent ones.

2

Positive Reinforcement

Always reward good behavior immediately. Use treats, praise, or play as motivation for your pet.

3

Patience and Persistence

Every pet learns at their own pace. Stay patient and keep training sessions positive and fun.

4

End on a High Note

Always finish training sessions with a successful command to keep your pet motivated for next time.
